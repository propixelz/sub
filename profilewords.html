About Twittedy

Twittedy is an experimental tool that explores the identity behind Twitter accounts.

It uses word clouds to visualize the most frequently occurring words in the biographies of those following an account, or the accounts followed.

The word clouds often reflect and reveal insightful underlying interests of the root account.

Twittedy needs to work with Twitter's API, which requires authentication. It respects your account by not sending surprise Tweets, or interfering with any Twitter data.
